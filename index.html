Detailed Materials

Create games and worlds with Real time light probes using our fast Temporal AA, SSAA, MSAA and Temporal Upsacle Screen Space Relfection technology.

OBJECTS AND GENERATION

Procedural Meshes

Scenes with massive amounts of geometric objects are no problem for our Engine, because of integrated procedural textures and meshes.

Our engine inprocesses only the detail you need, and loads them with our chache system.

EFFECTS

Particle System

The Curl Noise generated CPU and GPU based Particles are rendered smoothly and takes minimum computing power.

These Particles can be created with various tools such as Blender or manually created in our JSON format.
